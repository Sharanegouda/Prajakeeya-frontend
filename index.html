About the leader

A movement born from the people's frustration

Upendra Rao stepped beyond cinema to champion a new way of governance — one where citizens lead the system, not politicians. Prajakeeya is not just a political movement; it's a people-powered platform built on transparency, accountability, and public participation.

Believing that democracy should extend beyond elections, Upendra Rao united thinkers, technologists, social workers, and responsible citizens to create Prajakeeya — a platform where every individual can participate in shaping policies and solving real-world problems.

Our mission

Transforming how Karnataka is governed

I

Citizens as governors

Move beyond the ballot box — citizens actively shape policy, report problems, and vote on budgets all year round.

II

Every elected representative is rated publicly. Every fund is tracked. Every promise is timestamped.

III

Technology for everyone

From Bengaluru techies to Bidar farmers — the platform works for every Kannadiga.
