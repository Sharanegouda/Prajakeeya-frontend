🌧️ RED ALERT — Heavy rainfall, Dakshina Kannada
🔴 RED ALERT — EXTREME RAINFALL
Issued by: Karnataka Disaster Management Authority + IMD Bengaluru · Jun 21, 2024

The India Meteorological Department has issued a Red Alert for extremely heavy rainfall in Dakshina Kannada, Udupi, and Kodagu districts from June 21–23. Isolated rainfall of 20+ cm expected in 24 hours.

⚠️
Immediate actions required:
  • Residents in low-lying areas must evacuate to designated relief camps
  • Avoid river banks, coastal areas and hilly slopes
  • All schools, colleges and non-essential offices closed June 21–22
  • Motorists advised against travel on ghat roads
